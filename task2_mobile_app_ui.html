4. Confirmation

Design Rationale

Target UsersUrban women and men aged 18–40 in Pune who book beauty services digitally. They expect fast booking (under 60 seconds), real-time availability, and instant confirmation.
Booking Flow Logic4 taps to book: Home → Browse → Pick stylist + date + time → Confirm. Progressive disclosure keeps each screen focused. Progress bar shows users where they are.
UX DecisionsSticky "Confirm Booking" button avoids scroll back. Greyed-out unavailable slots eliminate confusion. Specialist selector with avatars adds trust and personalization.
Conversion Improvementsvs. phone/WhatsApp booking: always-on availability display, no wait for confirmation call, SMS receipt, calendar sync, and instant rebooking from profile history.
